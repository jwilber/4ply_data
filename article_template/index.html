    Data & Methodology


    Although TEMPLATE has had clips in many skate videos, the set of data was limited to include only his full-length video parts, as these are generally what we consider when looking at the "career" of a skater. Skate video information was (painstakingly) logged by-hand from the aforementioned videos. Data in the first chart was limited to tricks with counts greater than four. Data was limited to counts greater than two in the by-obstacle count charts. Data in the time-series chart was limited to ledges, handrails, and stairs/gaps because other obstacle classifications (e.g. transition, manuals) had too little counts for meaningful input.
